About

QUENCH is the social network designed to help you stay hydrated and connect with others who care about their health and wellness. We believe that water is the key to a healthier life, and with QUENCH, you can track your hydration goals, share your progress, and motivate others to drink more water every day.


Whether you're looking for tips, inspiration, or just a community to support your hydration journey, QUENCH is here to keep you refreshed and on track. It's more than just about drinking water – it's about creating a lifestyle that prioritizes well-being, one sip at a time.

About ME

About this Logo

This logo is a personal tribute to two things I really like: Star Wars and corvids. I really liked Sith Lord Revan when Star Wars Legends was still canon.


“Raven” is jus an uncreative play on words that to me represents the character and the bird. The number 66 comes from the iconic Order 66, the tragic massacre of the Jedi Order.


I placed them on cards as a reference to the many turn-based card games I’ve played and loved, The Logo has an overall simple meaning, with no cohesive connection to everything Iv'e mentioned so far.
